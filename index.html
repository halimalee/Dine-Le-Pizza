About Our Pizza

Founded in 2020 by Beryl Negesa. Our pizza hub has been serving up rich pizza sourced locally in various regions of Kenya. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best products, and bringing back to you here in our hub.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our decadent menu from the moment you walk inside until you finish your last bite. Join us for your daily snack, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.

Every bite of our quality pizza starts with locally sourced, hand picked ingredients. Once you try it, it will be a blissful addition to your everyday snacking routine - we guarantee it! Our innovation and creativity has enabled us to offer the widest range of pizzas allowing our customers to choose from our range of classic,favorite, signature and something special pizzas.Our pizzas are made with thin crusts, lots of toppings, and the perfect blend of sauces.Dine Le Pizza gives each and every customer quality and value for their money.But, that’s not all. We also offer great side orders like potato wedges, chicken wings and pop-itz.And of course, we have our unforgettable hot subs - a French baguette filled with mozzarella cheese and toppings of your choice.Dine Le Pizza - Try Something Amazing.
